The Process

From Owning a Job to Owning a System

No jargon. No long proposals. A straightforward, 90-day path that gets your time back and makes your money work for you.

Four milestones. Ninety days. Your life back.

Milestone 1 — Where Your Time & Money Actually Go

We calculate your true hourly rate and map where every hour goes. You'll see exactly what your busywork is costing — and the number usually surprises people.

Milestone 2 — What Stays, What Goes

Every task gets sorted into four buckets: keep (work you love and are great at), delegate (what drains you), automate (low-value but necessary), and delete (pure waste).

Milestone 3 — Your Systems Blueprint

We prioritize the biggest wins, design the systems to replace you in the wrong work, and build a 90-day roadmap with clear ROI on every change.

Milestone 4 — Handoff & Launch

We implement, document, and hand off. You train your team — or we do. Then you start getting your weeks back.
